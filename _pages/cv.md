Research Interests
======
My current research focuses on flexible medical robots (surgical robots) and human-centered wearable rehabilitation assistive robots, including supernumerary robotic limbs (SRLs), exoskeletons, and other bio-inspired robots. My research lies in mechanism design, optimization, and learning-based control.
